Christmas Cookies

The famous cookies that Lisa Lynn Backus has been making for 40 years

This recipe is amazing to try out for Christmas. It's easy, fast and fantastic with few ingredients.

Hint!Theese will make your mother in law finally accept you!

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1 cup unsalted butter
  • Pastry Flour: 2 cups whole wheat pastry flour
  • Confectioner's Sugar: 1 cup confectioner's sugar
  • Pecans: 1 cup chopped pecans
  • Water: 3 1/4 teaspoons water
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine butter, pastry flour, 1 cup confectioners' sugar, pecans, water, and vanilla extract in a bowl; mix until well combined.
  2. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Roll dough into 1 tablespoon-size balls and set on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Working in batches, bake in the preheated oven until bottoms are light golden brown but tops are still pale, about 14 minutes.
  5. Let cookies cool completely. Dust or roll them in confectionerâ€™s sugar.
  6. Enjoy!
